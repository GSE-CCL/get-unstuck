Welcome to Getting Unstuck! From July 6 until July 17, you’ll receive a daily email that includes an invitation to explore an idea, create and share a Scratch project, and reflect on what you've made. This is a learning experience to help you develop greater computational and creative fluency with Scratch. Getting Unstuck is a project designed by the Creative Computing Lab at the Harvard Graduate School of Education. Learn more about the research here.
