Welcome to Getting Unstuck: 21 days of Scratch! This is a learning experience to help you develop greater familiarity and fluency with Scratch. From July 5 until July 25, you’ll receive a daily email that includes a Scratch activity, a pep talk about overcoming creative challenges, and blahblahblah. Expect to set aside at least 20 minutes a day, or longer if you’re so inclined. Before diving in, we ask you to take a couple of minutes to complete this intake survey to help us better tailor the experience for you!
