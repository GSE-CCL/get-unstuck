Welcome to Getting Unstuck: 21 Days of Scratch! This is a learning experience to help you develop greater familiarity and fluency with Scratch. From July 5 until July 25, you’ll receive a daily email that includes a Scratch activity and a pep talk about overcoming creative challenges. Expect to set aside at least 20 minutes a day. (Or longer, if you’re so inclined!) Before diving in, we ask you to please take a couple of minutes to complete this intake survey to help us better understand you!

Getting Unstuck is a project designed by Paulina Haduong and Karen Brennan of the Creative Computing Lab at the Harvard Graduate School of Education.
