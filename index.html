Getting Unstuck

JULY 5 - JULY 25, 2018

21 days. 21 Scratch projects. What will you make?

Dear friends,
Supporting creative work in the classroom is hard, and we want to help you! We're excited to announce a summer learning experience for teachers who want to learn more about supporting creative work with the Scratch programming language.

Every day, you'll get an email with a mini programming challenge and a creative pep talk, designed to help you explore different computational concepts and problem-solving strategies in Scratch. You'll have access to a supportive online community of teachers and facilitators. We'll be right beside you, making things and learning together. Whether you're brand new to Scratch or an experienced teacher, we're excited to work together.

- Karen and Paulina
